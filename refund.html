Refund Policy

Last updated: October 28, 2025

Thank you for shopping at Mahin Ltd. We appreciate the trust you place in us and our digital products.

1. Digital Products Policy

As our products (such as software, app source codes, and digital tools) are delivered instantly and are intangible, we have a strict no-refund policy. Once a digital product has been purchased and delivered (e.g., via download link), we cannot offer a refund.

All sales are final. We encourage you to review all product descriptions, features, and screenshots before making a purchase.

2. Exceptions

We believe in customer satisfaction and will consider exceptions to this policy on a case-by-case basis under the following conditions:

  • Product Not as Described: If the product is demonstrably different from its description or preview.
  • Non-Delivery: If you did not receive the product download link after purchase.
  • Duplicate Purchase: If you accidentally purchased the same product twice.

To be eligible for a refund under these exceptions, you must contact us within 7 days of your purchase.

3. How to Request a Review

If you believe you are eligible for a refund based on the exceptions above, please contact our support team.

We will require documentation (such as screenshots or error messages) to assess your claim. We reserve the right to reject any refund request that does not meet our exception criteria.
